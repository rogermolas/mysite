Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

QRNest respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how QRNest handles information when you use the app.

Information Collection

QRNest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

We do not collect:

  • Names or contact details
  • Email addresses
  • Financial or payment information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

Data Storage

All QR codes saved in QRNest are stored locally on your device only.

QRNest does not use cloud storage or external servers. Your QR codes remain fully under your control and are never uploaded or shared unless you explicitly choose to share them using your device’s built-in sharing features.

Camera and Photo Library Access

QRNest may request access to:

  • Camera – to scan QR codes
  • Photo Library – to import or save QR images

These permissions are used only for core app functionality. No images or data are collected or transmitted by QRNest.

Third-Party Services

QRNest does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

QRNest does not sell, trade, or share any user data because no data is collected.

Children’s Privacy

QRNest does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, security is handled by your device’s operating system protections.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
