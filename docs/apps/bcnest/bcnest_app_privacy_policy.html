BCNest Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

BCNest ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BCNest uses your information, what permissions the app requires, and how your data is stored.

1. Information We Collect

BCNest does not collect, transmit, or sell your personal information.

All business cards, contact information, and other data you create or scan remain stored locally on your device.

2. Camera Permission

BCNest requests access to your device's camera solely to:

  • Scan QR codes containing digital business cards.
  • Import contact information from supported QR codes.

Camera images and video are never uploaded, stored remotely, or shared with us.

3. Local Data Storage

BCNest is designed to work completely offline.

Your business cards, scanned contacts, and app settings are stored locally on your device using on-device storage.

We do not maintain cloud servers or online databases containing your information.

4. Sharing Your Information

BCNest only shares your information when you explicitly choose to do so. Examples include:

  • Displaying your QR code for someone else to scan.
  • Sharing your digital business card.

No information is shared automatically.

5. Internet Connectivity

Most features of BCNest work without an internet connection.

Internet access may only be required for opening external links that you choose, such as websites or social media profiles contained in a business card.

6. Data Security

Because your information remains on your own device, you maintain control over your data.

While we take reasonable measures to design the application securely, no method of electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. Data Deletion

You may delete your business cards and contacts at any time within the app.

Uninstalling BCNest or clearing the application's data will permanently remove all locally stored information. This data cannot be recovered.

8. Children's Privacy

BCNest is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
