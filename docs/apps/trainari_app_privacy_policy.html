Trainari Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

Trainari (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Trainari collects, stores, and uses information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

Trainari does not collect any personal information on our servers. All information you enter in the app, such as:

  • Client details (name, address, contact information, notes)
  • Session schedules
  • Client profile pictures (stored locally from your Photo Library)

is stored locally on your device using Core Data. No client or personal data is transmitted to or stored on any remote server.

2. Permissions

  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select and store client profile pictures.
  • Notification Access: To schedule local reminders for upcoming sessions. All notifications are handled locally; no notification data is sent to remote servers.

3. Local Notifications

Trainari uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming sessions. These notifications are scheduled on your device only. No notification data is sent to remote servers.

4. Use of Your Data

The information stored locally is used solely to:

  • Manage and organize your clients
  • Schedule and remind you of training sessions

The data remains on your device and is not shared with third parties.

5. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device.
  • We recommend securing your device with a passcode or biometric lock to protect your information.

6. Third-Party Services

Trainari does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking services. Your information remains private and local.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be reflected in the app, and the effective date will be revised.
