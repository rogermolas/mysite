Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Thank you for using BudgetNest (BudNest). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how BudgetNest collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

BudgetNest is designed with privacy in mind.

Financial Data

All budgets, expenses, categories, and other financial information you create are stored locally on your device only.

BudgetNest does not upload, store, or synchronize your financial data to any external servers.

Analytics

BudgetNest uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage information that helps improve the app.

Analytics may include:

  • App launches
  • Screen views
  • Feature usage
  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • General performance information

This information is anonymous and is used solely to improve BudgetNest. It does not include your budgets, expenses, notes, or other financial data.

Data Storage

Your financial data remains on your device.

Because BudgetNest does not provide cloud backup or account synchronization:

  • Deleting the app permanently deletes all locally stored financial data.
  • Resetting your device or clearing the app's storage may also permanently remove your data.
  • You are responsible for maintaining backups available through your device or operating system.

BudgetNest cannot recover deleted financial data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal financial data with third parties.

Anonymous analytics data is processed by Firebase Analytics in accordance with Google's privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

BudgetNest uses the following third-party service:

  • Firebase Analytics (Google) – Anonymous application analytics.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

BudgetNest is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Since your financial data is stored locally on your device, protecting access to your device is important. If enabled, BudgetNest supports device authentication features such as Face ID, Touch ID, or a passcode to help secure your information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date shown above.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact us at:

Email: contact@brisklabs.dev

Summary

  • Your financial data stays on your device.
  • BudgetNest does not upload or store your budgets online.
  • Deleting the app permanently removes your locally stored data.
  • Firebase Analytics is used only for anonymous usage analytics.
