BShare (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how BShare collects, uses, and stores your information.

1. Permissions

  • Camera Access: BShare requires access to your device’s camera only to scan QR codes for sharing or adding contacts.
  • No other device data or personal information is collected.

2. Offline Usage

BShare works fully offline. Your digital business cards and scanned contacts are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on your device securely and privately. If you delete the app or clear its data, all stored information will be permanently lost.

4. Data Sharing

BShare does not share your personal data with anyone. Sharing of contact information only happens when you choose to share your card via QR code.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure your data stays private and protected on your device. However, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be reflected in the app or on our website.
